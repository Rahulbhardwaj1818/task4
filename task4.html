*Terms and conditions, also known as terms of service or terms of use, are a legal agreement outlining the rules and guidelines for using a website, app, or service. They establish a contract between the service provider and the user, defining acceptable behavior, prohibited actions, and the provider's rights. Essentially, they are a set of rules that both parties agree to abide by.Common clauses found in T&C include sections on user responsibilities, intellectual property, payment terms, limitation of liability, and dispute resolution. They also typically include an introduction, how acceptance of terms is indicated, and a clause on amendments.
* A reference to the Privacy Policy and a termination clause are often included as well.
*User behavior: They define what users can and cannot do while using the service, including acceptable use policies.
Intellectual property: They often address how users can use the service's content, including copyright and trademark information.
* Disclaimers: They may include disclaimers of warranties, limitations on liability, and other legal disclaimers to protect the service provider. Payment and subscriptions: They can specify how payments are handled, subscription terms, and related details.
* Account management: They may detail how user accounts can be created, managed, and terminated.
* Dispute resolution: They can outline the procedures for resolving disputes between the user and the service provider.
* Governing law: They specify which jurisdiction's laws govern the agreement.
